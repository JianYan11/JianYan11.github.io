XTION-YeYu 夜语

As a co-founder, participated in the World's First Haunted House Hackathon and developed "YeYu" (夜语) — an AI-powered emotional companion WeChat mini-program. The platform features anonymous emotional expression, AI-driven ProactiveCare for mental health support, and emotion resonance matching that connects users experiencing similar feelings. Built with privacy-first design using one-way hashing for complete anonymity. Tech stack includes FastAPI, FAISS, LangChain, and WeChat Mini Program.
作为联合创始人参加全球首届鬼屋黑客松，开发"夜语"——一款 AI 情感陪伴微信小程序。平台支持匿名情绪表达、AI 主动关怀 (ProactiveCare) 心理健康支持，以及情绪共鸣匹配，连接有相似感受的用户。采用隐私优先设计，使用单向哈希实现完全匿名。技术栈包括 FastAPI、FAISS、LangChain 和微信小程序。
